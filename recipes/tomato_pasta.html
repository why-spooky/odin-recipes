Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1.2kg of cherry tomoatoes
  • 120grams of goats cheese
  • 500grams of penne pasta
  • 2 tbl spoon
  • 4 large cloves of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of blackpepper
  • 1/4 cup of good quality olive oil
  • 1/4 cup beer (golden ale)
  • 6 leaves of fresh basil minced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 deg F
  2. Add the cherry tomatoes, garlie, goat cheese, salt and pepper to a backing dish, drizzle with olive oil
  3. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the tomatoes are bursting
  4. Remove from the over, stir in the beer
  5. Add the pasta, stir untill well coated, sprinkle with basil
  6. Plate, serve warm
