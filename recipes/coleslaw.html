Coleslaw

A tangy refreshing coleslaw!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of mayonaise
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp. Dijon mustard
  • 2 tsp. granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp. celery seed
  • Kosher Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 large head of green cabbage. Thinly sliced
  • 3 large carrots, grated

Instructions

  1. Whisk together mayonaise, vinegar, dijon mustard, sugar, and celery seed. Season with sald and pepper to taste. Add cabbage and carrots and mix thoroughly combine.
  2. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve
